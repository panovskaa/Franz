About

Franz opened on March 20th, 2026 — the result of over half a year of doubt, rebuilding, and starting over. A space that lived in someone's head for a long time before it became real.

Built for listening, lingering, and good company. Everything that makes us happy, all under one roof. Music, drinks, plates, people — no rush, no noise, just the right kind of atmosphere.

This is a personal place. It exists because of the people who believed in it before it had walls. We don't take that lightly.

Music is not background.
